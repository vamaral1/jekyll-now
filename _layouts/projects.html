Gesture-based control of ArDrone

I have implemented a closed loop system to control a simple quadcopter using gestures. I used the commercially available Parrot ArDrone 2 and ROS. Using an Asus Xtion Pro Live depth sensor, I was able to track certain arm movements and map them to specific controls that were sent to the quadcopter over Wifi.
